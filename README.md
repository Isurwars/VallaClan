# VallaClan

Welcome to the official repository hub for the **Computational Materials Science Group** at the Instituto de Investigaciones en Materiales (IIM) and the Facultad de Ciencias, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM).

This workspace hosts shared source codes, utility scripts, data analysis pipelines, and structural refinement protocols utilized and developed by our research cluster.

## 🛠 Featured Software & Tools

* **Correlation**: An advanced analysis tool designed for calculating the Pair Distribution Functions (PDFs) and Plane Angle Distributions (PADs) in amorphous solids, liquids, and high-entropy alloy simulations.
* **Velocities**: An in-house utility package used to generate customized initial random atomic velocity distributions for molecular dynamics (MD) workflows.
* **Simulation Hub**: Custom scripts and input templates for accelerating *ab initio* MD and Density Functional Theory (DFT) calculations using suites like Materials Studio (DMol3, CASTEP), LAMMPS, and Quantum Espresso on high-performance supercomputing clusters (such as Miztli).

## 🔬 Core Research Focus

Our group blends quantum mechanics, statistical mechanics, and high-performance computing to investigate:
* **Liquid Metals & Melts**: Phase transformations, topological properties, and short-range ordering (e.g., Liquid Bismuth).
* **Amorphous Solids & Alloys**: Structural disorder, metallic glasses, high-entropy systems, and their propensity to superconduct.
* **Low-Dimensional Systems**: Structural and electronic evolution of bismuth bilayers, 2D materials, and porous networks.
